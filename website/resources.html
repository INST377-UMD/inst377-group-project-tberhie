COVID Testing

If you are experiencing symptoms of COVID-19, have come into contact with someone who has COVID-19, have recently traveled to a densely populated area, or have traveled to an area with high COVID-19 infection rates, it is important to test for COVID-19. Luckily, there are multiple ways to get tested.
